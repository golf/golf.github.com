author: Micha Niskin
---

1. Download the Golf zip file.

** "golf.zip":http://cloud.github.com/downloads/golf/golf/golf.zip

2. Unzip the zip file into a directory in your path (e.g. @~/bin@).

That's all. Now you can open a terminal and do:

bc. golf --help

This will print the Golf command line usage and options page.
